About Me:


I am Dalton Okeefe, the owner and operator of Okeefe's Electrical. I took a great passion for electrical installation at a young age with a family history of electrical knowledge. Having served in the United States Army traveling to vast communities gave me a sense of fulfillment that I carry with me today. In combination with 13+ years of electrical installation and service experience in residential and commercial applications, I have a vision of growth and sustainability for our community.
